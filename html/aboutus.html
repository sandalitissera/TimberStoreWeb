Our Story

For over four decades, Sudharma Timber Store has been at the forefront of sustainable wood sourcing and premium timber products. What started as a small family business in 1985 has grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving contractors, architects, and DIY enthusiasts across the region.

Founded by craftsman Sudharma Perera, our company was built on the principles of quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Today, we continue to honor that legacy while embracing modern techniques and eco-friendly practices.
